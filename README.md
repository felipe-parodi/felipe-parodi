# Felipe Parodi
**Neuroscience × AI · Penn AI Fellow**

I study how primate brains encode social intelligence and how those
principles can inform artificial ones. My work combines neuroscience,
machine learning, and animal behavior to model social cognition from
neural, video, and audio data — and to probe how artificial systems
represent the world.
